Arnold Schwarzenegger

The inspirational story of Arnold Schwarzenegger's life

Timeline of Arnold Schwarzenegger's life:

  • 1947 - Born in Thal, Austria
  • 1960 - Arnold began weight training when his football coach took his team to a local gym.
  • 1961 - The former Mr. Austria Kurt Marnul invites Anrold to train with him at the gym in Graz.
  • 1965 - Serves one year mandatory service at the Austrian army. During this time he went AWOL to compete and win the Junior Mr. Europe contest.
  • 1966 - Places second at the NABBA Mr. Universe competition in London
  • 1967 - Becomes youngest ever Mr. Universe at the age of 20. He would go on to win the title a further three times.
  • 1968 - Moves to the US.
  • 1970 - Wins his first Mr. Olympia title in New York, and would go on to win the title a total of seven times. In that same year he had his first acting rule in Hercules in New York.
  • 1982 - His breakthrough film was Conan the Barbarian, which was a box-office hit.
  • 1991 - His commercial peak was his return as the title character in Terminator 2: Judgment Day, which was the highest-grossing film of the year.
  • 1993 - the National Association of Theatre Owners named him the "International Star of the Decade".
  • 2003 - Becomes governor of California.
  • 2011 - Just weeks after leaving office in California, Arnold returns to acting.
